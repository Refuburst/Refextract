Abstract
A measurement of the Higgs boson mass is presented based on the combined data
samples of the ATLAS and CMS experiments at the CERN LHC in the H → γγ and
H → ZZ → 4(cid:96) decay channels. The results are obtained from a simultaneous fit to
the reconstructed invariant mass peaks in the two channels and for the two experi-
ments. Themeasuredmassesfromtheindividualchannelsandthetwoexperiments
are found to be consistent among themselves. The combined measured mass of the
Higgsbosonism = 125.09±0.21(stat.)±0.11(syst.)GeV.
